Abstraction is when you take a complex system, give it a name, dealing with it as a whole and don't give much concern to its details.

Example

- A name or a title of someone is an Abstraction of that human, this name or title hide a lot of complex details that arises from the fact that he is a human with a mind, skin, bones, respiratory system, ... etc. So we think of him as john "The Instructor" and never bother ourselves with how this systems works or how many carbon atoms john have.
